
A remix of https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:6476036 by ethomasgt.

Enhancements:

Added support to eliminate flexing
Lifted the tool head to reduce lever effects
Shifted the CG towards the rail to reduce the overhang sags.
Added mounting point for BTT PAD7 ADXL345 module that has a 44mm width mounting point on top of the linear rail.
These enhancements effectively reduce the Z offset caused by sagging under heavy toolhead loads and reduce vibration during rapid accelerations. This bracket should keep your Z offset stable between -2.0 and -2.7mm, depending on the material used and print strength.
